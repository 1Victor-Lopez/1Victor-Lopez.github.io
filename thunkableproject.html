The overall purpose of my program is to mimic what an ATM would regularly do. The video shows the user entering their account information and managing to enter it. When they enter, they deposit money into the account. In order to enter your account, you have to put in your personal information. When entered, you deposit the amount you wish by writing in the text box following the instructions. After confirming the message box, the new total balance is in the account. You can also click on the “More Info” box on the main screen to see more information about the app.
